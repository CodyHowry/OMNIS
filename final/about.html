About OmnisPC

About Us

OmnisPC is a Lancaster, PA based company with a team of learning college students dedicated to providing premium quality computer repair and support services to homes and businesses locally. We use this business as a way for enrolled college students in the IT field to get a better understanding of the troubleshooting process. It is our mission to deliver your product in a better state than what you dropped it off in, while allowing students to advance their various skills.
